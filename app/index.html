How it works

Sense, forecast, act

A process-based twin of the pond, calibrated to Vietnamese vannamei farms.

Sense

Probes stream oxygen, pH, temperature, ammonia and salinity from the pond.

Forecast

Coupled-ODE twin rolls the pond forward and predicts the overnight oxygen crash hours ahead.

Act

It drives aeration the moment the forecast nears the stress line, so the crash never happens.
